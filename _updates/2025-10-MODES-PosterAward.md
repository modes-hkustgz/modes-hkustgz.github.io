On October 29, 2025, the first *INTR Academic Star Gathering – Spotlight Poster Event* was successfully held at iPark. Seven students (six PhD students and one Master’s student) presented their research posters showcasing the latest progress of their work.

The results were announced on October 31. **Miaowei Xi** and **Wenjie Zhu** received the **only First Prize**, the highest prize below the Grand Prize. **Haoyu Mo** was among the three winners of the **Second Prize**, while **Manlian Pan, Xinzhu Ren, and Chenglin Zhuang** received the **Third Prize**. **Junying Ma** received the **Excellence Award**. 

Congratulations to all awardees for their excellent presentations and contributions!
